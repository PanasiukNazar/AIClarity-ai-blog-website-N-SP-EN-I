1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help recognize your browser, remember preferences, and improve website functionality. Cookies do not store personal data but may be linked to other information we collect about you.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

These cookies are necessary for website functionality, including security and navigation.

3.4. Analytics Cookies
